### Saddle River Day School — RTU Installation

> Today we successfully received and completed the installation of five (5) Greenheck high-efficiency rooftop units (RTUs) for our ongoing project at Saddle River Day School. The RTUs were delivered, rigged, and set in place in coordination with the project team, marking a major milestone in the mechanical construction phase. These units will serve as the primary air handling systems for the building and are equipped with energy recovery wheels to support high indoor air quality and energy-efficient operation throughout classrooms, laboratories, and common spaces. ## FAQ (6)

1. **What types of commercial HVAC systems do you install?**
   We install all major commercial HVAC systems, including rooftop units, chillers, boilers, and ducted or ductless systems. Whether it's a high-rise, office building, or multi-unit residential complex, we design and install energy-efficient systems built for performance and longevity.

2. **Do you offer regular commercial HVAC maintenance?**
   Yes! We provide flexible commercial HVAC maintenance services to keep your system running efficiently year-round. Our technicians perform routine inspections, tune-ups, and cleaning to prevent breakdowns and extend equipment life—so you can focus on managing your building, not chasing repairs.

3. **How fast can you respond to HVAC repair emergencies?**
   We offer 24/7 emergency HVAC repair across Brooklyn and the greater NYC area. Whether it's a failing rooftop unit or an issue with airflow, our trained techs are on-call to diagnose and fix problems fast, day or night.

4. **Which HVAC brands do you service and install?**
   We work with trusted commercial HVAC brands like Carrier, Trane, Goodman, Amana, Bryant, Daikin, Mitsubishi, York, Lennox, Rheem, Samsung, and more. Our team is trained to install, repair, and maintain systems from all major manufacturers.

5. **Where do you provide commercial HVAC services?**
   We proudly serve Brooklyn, all five NYC boroughs, New Jersey, and Connecticut. From Midtown office towers to Brooklyn brownstones, we've got your building's HVAC needs covered.

6. **Do you offer HVAC services for new construction projects?**
   Absolutely. We specialize in commercial HVAC design and installation for new builds. Whether it's a residential development or a commercial space, we partner with contractors and developers to build HVAC systems that meet code, budget, and timeline.
